About EES

Electrical Engineering society, BIT Sindri is a society for electrical engineers. It's the community,responsible for carrying out the various welfare programs for the students of the department. The society is made up of a varied collection of individuals, working together to organize events beyond the syllabus and curriculum by arranging workshops, expert talks and industrial visits.

Vid-Youth

"VID-YOUTH" the mega event of Electrical Engineering Society (EES) provides a platform to the young minds to showcase their ideas innovations,skills,presentation.The three segements of VID-YOUTH includes Model United Nations(MUN), Master of Minds(MOM) and SHRUSHTI. It aims for environmental sustainability by promoting and creating awareness regarding renewable energy resources.
